PayScope

Scoped payment authorization for AI agents on Ethereum.
Agents spend within limits — enforced by smart contract, not trust.

🏆 The Synthesis 2026 ⛓️ Sepolia Testnet 🤖 OpenClaw + Claude

The Problem

No spending limits

AI agents with wallet access can drain your entire balance — there's nothing stopping them.

No purpose enforcement

An agent authorized to pay for "cloud services" can just as easily pay for anything else.

No audit trail

You can't verify what was spent, when, or why — until it's too late.

The Solution

💰 Scoped budgets

Fund a scope with exactly the ETH you want an agent to spend. Contract enforces the limit.

🎯 Purpose binding

Agents can only spend for the declared purpose. "cloud services only" means exactly that.

📋 On-chain audit

Every payment is logged on-chain with a memo. Immutable, verifiable, permanent.

🔒 Owner control

Revoke any scope instantly. Unspent ETH returned immediately. No lockup.
